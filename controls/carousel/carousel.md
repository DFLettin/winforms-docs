__RadCarousel__ is a navigation control that animates a series of elements either by the user clicking a particular element or by clicking the forward and back arrows. Based on top of the Telerik Presentation Framework (TPF), __RadCarousel__ supports data binding, smooth animations and transitions, automatic generation of image reflections and dynamic addition and removal of items.Â
